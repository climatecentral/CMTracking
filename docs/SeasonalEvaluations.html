Seasonal Evaluations

Last updated: 12/15/2020

Seasonal evaluations are quarterly internal reports that look back on the releases published by meteorological season in order to gain insights into what the most popular topics were across our programs, across media platforms, which assets performed well and which content was less popular.

If you’re unsure about release or graphic names, click here to find a guide, under ‘Graphics Directory’ tab.

Fall 2020

Fall content is considered to be all weekly releases and graphics published between ‘2020FallPackage’ and ‘2020SeasonalWarming’, inclusive.

Graphic Popularity by Release

Graphics are ordered by publication date then by popularity, to show the popularity of each graphic in a given release.

Graphic Popularity by Attribute

‘Attribute’ is used to describe two characteristics of a graphic: the geographic resolution, and the type of graphic. Graphic popularity is calculated as the number of hits per graphic of that attribute.

Graphic ‘type’ is classified as: chart, map, explainer, gif or other. Graphic ‘geo’ is classified as: local/state, regional, national, global or n/a.
